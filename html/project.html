Vadati - The Helping Hand

(Flex Sensor, Raspberry Pi,Kivy, Speech Recognition)

The helping hand assists specially abled people to live with normal people. As dumb people cannot speak this project will assist them to convert their hand gestures into pre-recorded voice or text. This will also give normal people an ability to understand what the person is trying to say and communicate accordingly. This will also give the facility controlling home appliance with which a physically impared person becomes independent to their life. The basic feature of the implemented project is to develop a dependable, easy to use system which can decrease the obstacles for disabled people where they can stand with the world.
